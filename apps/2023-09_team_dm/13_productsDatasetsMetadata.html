Products - Datasets & Metadata (Documentation)

Lifecycle elements: obtain/collect, store/backup, find, understand, document, report, preserve, share outside of team

  • Identify and use appropriate documentation standards (FAIR principles)
  • Identify target repository and associated requirements early (TRUST principles)
  • Plan for data transformation into preservation ready formats (e.g Library of Congress Sustainability of Digital Formats)
  • Select and attach an appropriate open access license (e.g. Creative Commons)
  • Comply with preservation, sharing, and documented limitations on sharing requirements (e.g. CARE principles, data use agreements, human subjects consent)
